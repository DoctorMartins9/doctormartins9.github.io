From January to July 2024 I was a visiting scholar at the Department of Mechanical Engineering and Applied Mechanics, University of Pennsylvania, in Philadelphia.

The visit focused on real-time vision-based collision prediction and avoidance for safe human-robot coexistence and physical assistance, and included an invited talk at the Figueroa Robotics Laboratory.
