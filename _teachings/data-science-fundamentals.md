## Overview

Adjunct teaching activity within the Department of Engineering for Innovation Medicine.

The course supports the BSc in Computer Engineering for Robotics and Intelligent Systems and focuses on programming foundations that are relevant for cyber-physical and robotic systems.

## Scope

- programming concepts for cyber-physical systems
- applied software development for robotics-oriented contexts
- lab-oriented teaching activity
