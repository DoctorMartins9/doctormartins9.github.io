This line of work focuses on extracting stable gait features from RGB and multi-view video, with enough robustness for clinical and telemedicine settings.
It connects pose estimation with validation on human data and practical downstream metrics, following the direction of {% cite martini2022enabling %} and {% cite martini2022real %}.

- markerless capture
- clinically meaningful kinematics
- remote assessment workflows
